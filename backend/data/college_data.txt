Marathwada Mitra Mandal’s College of Engineering (MMCOE), Pune is a well-known engineering institute offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The institute is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and is recognized by AICTE.

The college offers a variety of Undergraduate (UG) programs including:
- Computer Engineering  
- Information Technology  
- Mechanical Engineering  
- Electrical Engineering  
- Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering  
- Artificial Intelligence and Data Science  
- Engineering Science and Humanities  

Postgraduate (PG) programs include:
- MBA  
- Computer Engineering  
- Mechanical Engineering (Design Engineering)  
- Electronics and Telecommunication (VLSI & Embedded Systems)  
- Electrical Engineering (Power Electronics & Drives)  
- Information Technology (Data Science)  

The institute also offers Ph.D. programs in multiple disciplines for research and higher studies.

The admission process includes checking eligibility criteria, submitting required documents, and completing the fee structure as per guidelines. Admission-related information such as cut-offs, FAQs, and notices are available for students.

MMCOE has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell that provides placement support, internship opportunities, and connects students with top recruiters. The college focuses on skill development, industry interaction, and career readiness through structured training programs.

Facilities available at the college include:
- Library  
- Hostel  
- Mess and Canteen  
- Playground and Sports Facilities  
- Gymnasium  
- Counselling services  

The college actively organizes workshops, hackathons, conferences, and technical events to enhance student learning and innovation.

MMCOE also promotes industry-academia collaboration through MoUs, research initiatives, and student-centric activities to prepare students for real-world challenges.
